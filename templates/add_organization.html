What is an Organization?

An Organization is a parent company or group that has multiple branches.

Examples:
  • Corona Schools → Corona Lekki, Corona Gbagada, Corona VI
  • GTBank → GTBank Lagos, GTBank Abuja, GTBank Port Harcourt
  • Greensprings → Greensprings Lekki, Greensprings Ikoyi
Tip: After creating an organization, you can assign branches to it when adding or editing branches.
